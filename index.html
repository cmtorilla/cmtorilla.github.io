What is COVID-19 and how is the Philippines handling the situation?

Based on the World Health Organization statement, Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new strain of coronavirus. It has brought fear and anxiety to society as the cases keep rising in parts worldwide; my country, the Philippines, is part of that. The government has implemented several acts such as community quarantine implemented to the whole country and ran different testing labs tests. COVID ROTY is a non-profit organization led by a Pisay student in the Philippines. We are here to provide you our insights and sources of information regarding the pandemic. Stay safe and wash your hands!
